Sabrina Sommer is an art director, graphic designer, type designer, and digital expressionist living and working in Brooklyn, NY.

Currently she works as a designer at Wieden + Kennedy NYC. She is available for freelance work as well. In spare time she pursues personal projects and education mainly focusing in publication or type design. She recently completed Letterform Archive's Typewest program. She believes community is key. She runs the substack for Type Tonight NYC.
